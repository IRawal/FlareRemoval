Objective

ClearView is an innovative project dedicated to the development and implementation of advanced techniques for the removal of flare and streak light from visual media. Leveraging cutting-edge image processing algorithms and machine learning, ClearView aims to enhance the clarity and quality of images and videos by effectively reducing unwanted bright spots and reflections.

Enhanced Visual Quality

By removing glare and flare, ClearView significantly improves the clarity and detail of images and videos, making them more visually appealing and easier to analyze.

Professional Results

Enables photographers, videographers, and content creators to produce professional-grade visuals without the need for extensive post-processing.

Time and Cost Efficiency

Automates the process of glare and flare removal, saving time and reducing the need for manual editing, thereby lowering costs.
